
> Much of enterprise architecture is about understanding what is worth the costs of central coordination, and what form that coordination should take. At one extreme is a central architecture group that must approve all architectural decision for every software system in the enterprise. Such groups slow down decision making and cannot truly understand the issues across such a wide portfolio of systems, leading to poor decision-making. But the other extreme is no coordination at all, leading to teams duplicating effort, inability for different system to inter-operate, and a lack of skills development and cross-learning between teams.
>
> Sumber: https://martinfowler.com/architecture/

Catatan gue:

- Paragraf ini menutup dualisme enterprise architecture: sentralisasi penuh dan tanpa koordinasi sama-sama bermasalah.
- Ekstrem tanpa koordinasi menyebabkan duplikasi kerja, sistem tidak interoperable, dan hilangnya cross-learning antar tim.
- Fowler menolak romantisasi otonomi tanpa mekanisme berbagi standar dan pembelajaran.

Implikasi:

- Otonomi tanpa mekanisme berbagi standar dan pembelajaran akan berubah menjadi fragmentasi mahal.
- Arsitektur enterprise butuh koordinasi yang tepat, bukan nol koordinasi.
- Mekanisme koordinasi bisa berbeda bentuknya, tetapi harus ada cara untuk menjaga interoperabilitas dan transfer skill.
